LEADERSHIP & VISION

Meet Our Founder

Mrs. Lakshmi M N is the visionary founder and leader of PeeJay Hospitality Services. Establishing the organization in 2009 with a commitment to trust, reliability, and top-tier professionalism, she has built the enterprise from the ground up into one of Andhra Pradesh's most respected facility management providers.

Under her guidance, PeeJay Hospitality Services delivers comprehensive solutions—including high-quality corporate housekeeping, expert horticulture, landscaping, and reliable maintenance services—to over 50 corporate clients, ranging from leading IT parks and retail centers to healthcare facilities and educational institutions.

"We believe a clean, secure, and well-managed environment is foundational to productivity and business success. Our focus remains on delivering customized solutions, maintaining strict compliance standards, and nurturing long-term partnerships with our clients."

— Lakshmi M N, Founder & Managing Director
